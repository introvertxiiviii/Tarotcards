Suit of Wands

The Suit of Wands in tarot represents passion, inspiration, creativity, and personal growth. It is associated with the element of Fire, symbolizing energy, ambition, and enthusiasm. The Wands cards reflect our desires, ambitions, and the pursuit of our goals and dreams.

Ace of Wands

Represents new creative opportunities, inspiration, and the spark of potential. It signifies the beginning of a passionate endeavor or the awakening of a new creative project.
