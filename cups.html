Suit of Cups

The Suit of Cups in tarot represents emotions, relationships, and matters of the heart. It is associated with the element of water, symbolizing intuition, sensitivity, and the flow of emotions. The Cups cards reflect our emotional experiences, connections with others, and the exploration of our deepest feelings.

Suits OF Cups

Ace of Cups

Represents new beginnings in love, emotional fulfillment, and spiritual growth. It signifies the potential for deep emotional connections and a sense of overflowing love..

Two of Cups

Symbolizes harmony, balance, and partnership. It represents the union of two souls, whether in a romantic relationship or a deep friendship, highlighting mutual respect and emotional reciprocity.

Three of Cups

Signifies celebration, joy, and friendship. It represents coming together with loved ones, creating a sense of unity, and sharing happiness and abundance.

Four of Cups

Reflects contemplation, introspection, and a need for emotional evaluation. It suggests a time of reflection on one's emotions and a desire for something deeper or more fulfilling.

Five of Cups:

Represents loss, disappointment, and grief. It signifies the need to process and heal from emotional pain, while also reminding us to find hope and perspective amid challenging times.

Six of Cups

Symbolizes nostalgia, innocence, and happy memories. It evokes feelings of childlike joy, reconnecting with the past, and finding comfort in the simple pleasures of life.

Seven of Cups

Signifies choices, fantasies, and illusions. It represents a period of decision-making and the need to discern between realistic goals and fleeting desires or fantasies.

Eight of Cups

Reflects the act of walking away, leaving behind what no longer serves us emotionally. It signifies the search for deeper meaning, personal growth, and the courage to follow one's true path.

Nine of Cups

Known as the "Wish Card," it represents emotional fulfillment, contentment, and personal satisfaction. It signifies a time of abundance, happiness, and the realization of heartfelt desires.

Ten of Cups

Symbolizes ultimate emotional fulfillment, harmony, and family. It represents a deep sense of love, unity, and domestic bliss within relationships and the joy that comes from a strong support system.
